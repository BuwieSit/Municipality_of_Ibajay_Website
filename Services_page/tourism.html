Binakol na Manok

"Binakol na Manok" originates from the Western Visayas, particularly the province of Aklan. Binakoe (Binakol) is a Filipino chicken soup made from native chicken cooked in coconut water, with grated coconut, green papaya, leafy vegetables, and more.
